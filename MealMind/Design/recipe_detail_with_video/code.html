Healthy Tomato Egg Scramble

shopping_basket Ingredients

  • Large eggs
    4 units
  • Ripe tomatoes
    2 medium
  • Green onions
    2 stalks
  • Olive oil
    1 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper
    To taste

Family Tip

Add a teaspoon of honey while sautéing tomatoes to balance the acidity for kids!

skillet Preparation Steps

01

Prep the Base

Whisk the eggs in a small bowl with a pinch of salt. Dice the tomatoes into 1-inch chunks and thinly slice the green onions.

02

Sauté Tomatoes

Heat olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add tomatoes and sauté for 3-4 minutes until they soften and release their juices.

03

Scramble

Pour in the whisked eggs. Let them sit for 10 seconds, then gently fold them into the tomatoes until just set but still creamy.

04

Final Garnish

Remove from heat. Top with sliced green onions and extra black pepper. Serve immediately with warm sourdough toast.
